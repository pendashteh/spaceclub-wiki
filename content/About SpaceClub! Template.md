## What's special about the SpaceClub! Template
SpaceClub! is designed so you can own a good looking website that you can take anywhere and you can easily edit. Here is how:
### Take it Anywhere
SpaceClub! is a static site template. That means you can copy the template to your laptop, make changes, build, and then view your website!
It also means you can take your website anywhere, and there are a ton of easy and/or free options on where to put it (i.e. host) on the web. 

### Easy to Edit
Since the template is built using Eleventy, editing content is easy! It's not drag and drop but you also don't need to learn a bunch of HTML.
All the pages are easy to read and because it's markdown, you can type normal text and it will show on your website!
